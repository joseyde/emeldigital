Office 365

The Dock 365 Contract Management System (CMS) offers an effortless contract management life cycle platform. Dock CMS gives users the power to streamline the contract life cycle process on your SharePoint tenant. Dock CMS users can control and edit contracts, track obligations, set review dates, review all components of your vendor and customer contracts, and more.

How Dock CMS Works

Flexible Workflow

Design and automate agreement processes to eliminate bottlenecks and automate handoffs. Automated reminder notifications on your important contract milestones.

Centralized Repository

Create, store, sort, and manage contracts in one searchable place to increase visibility and find contracts faster. OCR capability to convert your old contract searchable.

Contract Generation

Create and store pre-built document templates that auto-populate contracts that allow contract creation in minutes.

Clause Library

Cut down on negotiation times by building storehouse of pre-approved clauses for sales teams.

Collaboration Tools

Review contracts, track changes across document versions, and keep a record of who performed what actions and when.

Efficient contract intake

Streamline your contract intake process with our a fully customizable intake process.

Vendor Portal

Store, track, and manage contract parties as well as view party contact information, contracts, and more.

Electronic Signature

Integrate with tools like DocuSign, AdobeSign and Sertifi out-of-the-box.

Secure Storage

Microsoft's secure cloud storage and permissions-based access allows you to keep your data safe.
