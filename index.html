UX Case Study

UX casestudy about medicine reminder app

This project was something we could relate to in our daily life. Nowadays most of our family members are used to taking medicines every day, especially old parents. But due to our fast life or lack of memory, most of the time we usually forgot to take our medicines on time. That may be the case with our lovely children or our parents who are living away from us. There are so many mobile applications available in the market for medicine reminders. But they are handling the individual personâ€™s cases only. Users may need to use different applications in case of an emergency or for taking doctor appointments or finding old prescriptions.







The Problem

  • Family members are taking medicines or supplements daily. They need to track it.
  • Grandparents or kids who are in remote locations may use to taking medicines. There is a high chance of misses in taking medicines
  • In case of an emergency individual person who is in remote or in travel may get difficulty contacting the emergency response team or family member
  • Itâ€™s too difficult to take online appointments to consult a doctor and keep all the prescriptions, especially in the case of old family members.




The Solution







Developed a mobile app in which users can control all medical-related activities of all the family members including the medicine-taking alarm even though they are in a remote location or in travel. Our main aim was to simplify the use of mobile applications for older people and give them a feeling of family caring for them even if they are alone at home.

The app can be used as two different profiles one is for the person who manage the main app and the second one is for the parent, child, or patient. In case they missed some alarms we have provided an option to get notified and there will be an option to make a video call in the system itself or can make an SOS alert.

We have also provided a couple of options like Doctor appointment booking, prescription management, custom alert management, SOS alert, insurance management, health progress etc..
